"MLA Hitendra Thakur Pushes for Vasai-Bhayandar Metro Extension to Enhance Connectivity"

MLA Hitendra Thakur is advocating for the extension of Mumbai's Metro Line 9 from Vasai to Bhayandar. This proposed extension aims to improve connectivity in the region, addressing transportation challenges and benefiting local residents. Thakur emphasizes that extending the metro line would enhance convenience and economic development in the area, offering a more efficient and reliable mode of transport. The proposal has received support from local communities and is seen as a crucial step toward better infrastructure in the region.
